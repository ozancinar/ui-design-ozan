The Safety Assessment Workflow

The Safety Assessment Workflow is a combination of tools and data selected and integrated to perform generic risk assessment. The Flow consists of five Steps.

The five Safety Assessment Workflow Steps

Each step is one of the components of the Safety Assessment Workflow and categorizes processes that users can perform with the Virtual Human Platform. Each step is named after a term from the VHP4Safety Glossary. The five Safety Assessment Workflow are not sequential.
